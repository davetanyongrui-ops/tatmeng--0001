Who We Are: Direct-Factory Carpentry Specialists

At Tat Meng, we bridge the gap between high-end design and precision fabrication. As a direct-factory carpentry specialist in Singapore, our mission is to deliver premium, bespoke woodwork without the interior design markups or middleman fees.

We believe every space, whether a compact HDB flat, a luxury condominium or a high-traffic commercial retail unit, deserves cabinetry that is as functional as it is visually striking.

Expertise

Residential Built-Ins & Commercial Fit-Outs

home_work

Built for Singapore Homes

For homeowners, we create space-efficient cabinetry for kitchens, wardrobes, TV consoles, foyers and home offices. Each design is planned around the room, appliance clearances and the storage you actually need.

Select laminates, internal configurations and hardware to suit your preferred look, daily usage and budget. We recommend suitable material options for Singapore's humid conditions and high-use areas.

storefront

Commercial Fit-Out Solutions

For commercial spaces, we fabricate reception counters, retail displays, workstations, pantry cabinets and F&B fixtures that support operations while presenting your brand cleanly.

Bring your floor plan, drawings or reference images. We review dimensions, material choices and installation constraints before fabrication, so the quoted scope stays clear.
