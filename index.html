Why Direct Factory?

Custom carpentry, minus the middleman.

At Tat Meng, you work directly with the people who build your carpentry. No middleman, no confusing markups, and no unnecessary back-and-forth.

Every project is made in our local Singapore workshop, giving you better control over materials, finishing, timeline, and budget. From measurement to fabrication to installation, our team handles the process directly — so you get practical advice, clear communication, and honest workmanship.
