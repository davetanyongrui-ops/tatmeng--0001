Visual Examples

Common Project Scopes

These examples show typical work categories. Your quotation is based on confirmed measurements, materials, hardware and installation needs.

01 / Residential

Residential Built-In Carpentry

Storage planned for HDB flats, BTO units, condominiums and landed homes, with layouts fitted to your daily use.

Custom Kitchen Cabinets

Moisture-resistant cabinetry planned around appliances, with seamless ABS trimming, customizable internal carcasses, and premium soft-close mechanics such as BLUM systems.

Wardrobes & Walk-In Closets

Floor-to-ceiling storage with sliding or casement doors, pull-out accessory trays, vanity mirrors, and sensor LED lighting.

Feature Walls & TV Consoles

Floating consoles, suspended shelves, hidden wire management, and large-format accent walls that anchor the living space.

Foyer & Shoe Cabinets

Space-saving, ventilated entry storage built to match your home's design language.

Study & Home Office Stations

Integrated long-span desks, heavy-load bookshelves, and overhead filing cabinets for clean, ergonomic workflows.

Bathroom Vanity Cabinets

High-moisture-resistant under-sink cabinetry with water-repellent laminates and clean plumbing concealment.

Platform Beds & Hidden Storage

Elevated bedroom systems with concealed lift-up hatches or hydraulic storage drawers, replacing bulky freestanding frames.

02 / Commercial

Commercial & Retail Interior Fit-Outs

Counters, displays and workplace cabinetry designed around customer flow, operations and your chosen finishes.

Reception Counters & Front Desks

Bespoke architectural statement counters using premium stone, wood veneer, or integrated laminate finishes.

Office Workstations & Discussion Pods

Ergonomic acoustic partitions, custom meeting tables, and collaborative desks tailored to corporate layouts.

Retail Display Units & Shelving

Heavy-load perimeter shelving, product islands, and glass-encased showcases for boutiques and galleries.

F&B Cafe & Restaurant Carpentry

Industrial bar counters, banquette seating structures, server stations, and architectural overhead wooden lattices.

Pantry & Office Lounge Assemblies

Lockers, kitchenettes, and durable breakroom counters engineered for frequent communal use.

03 / Made to Measure

Bespoke Standalone Furniture

Made-to-measure pieces for rooms, uses or dimensions that standard furniture cannot address well.

Sideboards & Credenzas

Sized to exact room specifications with drawers, shelving, and premium wood veneer or laminate finishes.

Display Cases & Bookshelves

Floor-to-ceiling libraries, collectible display nooks, and custom room-divider shelving.

Bed Frames & Headboards

Upholstered or geometric wood-slat headboards integrated into solid foundation platforms.

Accent Benches & Sideboards

Low-profile credenzas, custom entry consoles, and storage benches.

04 / Exterior

Structural Outdoor Carpentry

Outdoor structures and screens using material options selected for exposed Singapore conditions.

Timber & Composite Decking

High-performance, splinter-free decking for balconies, patios, and planter boxes.

Trellises & Pergolas

Solid timber overhead structures providing shade and structural character.

Privacy Fencing & Louvered Screens

Exterior screens that reduce direct sunlight and views while maintaining airflow.

05 / Aftercare

Repair, Restoration & Alteration Work

Targeted alterations and repairs that may extend the usable life of existing cabinetry.

Hardware Retrofitting & Replacements

Upgrade creaking hinges or broken drawer runners to quiet, premium soft-close systems.

Cabinet Resizing & Modifications

Re-engineer existing kitchens to accommodate larger ovens, dishwashers, or refrigerators.

Laminate Re-Skinning & Repairs

Repair water-logged cabinet bases, peeling laminate edges, and chipped surfaces.

Our Process

From Floor Plan to Finished Installation

Send the information we need to assess the job, then confirm the quoted scope before fabrication and installation.

  1. 01

    Send Your Brief

    Share your floor plan, inspiration images, dimensions, or repair photos through WhatsApp or email.

  2. 02

    Site Measurement

    We assess access, measurements, appliance clearances, storage needs, and installation constraints.

  3. 03

    Itemized Quotation

    You receive a clear quotation covering scope, materials, hardware, finishes, and installation.

  4. 04

    Material Confirmation

    Confirm laminates, timber tones, hardware systems, internal configurations, and final dimensions.

  5. 05

    Factory Fabrication

    Our workshop fabricates your carpentry with controlled cutting, assembly, and quality checks.

  6. 06

    Install & Handover

    Our team fits, adjusts, cleans up, and walks through the completed installation with you.
